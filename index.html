The Building

77 Hudson offers expansive views of the Manhattan skyline and the Statue of Liberty. What makes this building feel like home is our active community. Weekly social events and group classes bring neighbors together. Our 24-hr staff keeps everything on track. An owner's association board oversees 77 Hudson and FirstService Residential is our company of choice for property management.
